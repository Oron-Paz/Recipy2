How it Works:

Welcome to Recipy, your AI cooking assistant! Here's how it work:

  1. Your Preferences: Share your cravings with us. You have two options:
    • Ingredients: Tell us what ingredients you have in your kitchen, and we'll work our magic to create a recipe just for you.
    • Cooking Style or Mood: Not sure what you want? No problem. Tell us your current vibe - are you in the mood for comfort food, something spicy, or a healthy option?
  2. AI Magic: Our advanced AI, powered by OpenAI, analyzes your inputs and conjures a personalized recipe to satisfy your taste buds.
  3. Your Unique Recipe: Once processed your one-of-a-kind recipe is ready. Explore details, from ingredients to instructions, and nutritional info. If it doesn't quite hit the spot, we're here with another suggestion.
  4. Cook and Share: Follow the instructions to whip up your delectable meal. Whether you're a pro or novice, Recipy simplifies your culinary journey. Share your creations with friends and family.

Recipy, your culinary companion, simplifies the recipe discovery process. Bid farewell to endless searches and welcome a world of culinary possibilities.
