How It Works

  1. Tell Us What's in Your Kitchen

    Begin by sharing what you've got on hand or what's on your mind. Just type in the ingredients or dish you have in mind, and we'll do the rest.

  2. We Get Your Idea

    Our friendly AI gets to work and understands your request, just like a helpful kitchen assistant. It translates your words into a clear recipe hunt.

  3. Recipe Discovery, Made Easy

    No need to sift through countless cookbooks or websites. Recipy takes a quick peek in its recipe treasure chest and pulls out the most fitting options.

  4. Recipe Ideas, Served Fresh

    Voila! In a snap, we present you with a handful of personalized recipes that fit your description. Every recipe comes with step-by-step instructions, so you can start cooking with confidence.
